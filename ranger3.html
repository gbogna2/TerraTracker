Part Three: Evil Boss Melee

Revisiting The Evil Preparations Boss Fight #2

Next on the to-do list is the Evil Boss. The boss is different for both biomes, but will ultimately end up defeated.

Most of your preparations for your next fight involve expanding the width of the care you're fighting in. This will help you avoid incoming attacks.

To summon your next boss, you must destroy 3 Shadow Orbs. These orbs are spread out through the underground of the evil biomes.
