Part Nine: Plantera and Golem Ranger

Looming Evils in the Jungle Boss Fight #9 Needle and a Haystack Traversing the Temple Boss Fight #10

Your next fight will take place in the jungle. This fight might be a little difficult, but I have faith in you.

Plantera will move around with it's tendrils, and also mainly try to shoot projectiles at you. It's essential that you dodge these, as they will inflict debuffs on you.

You will obtain a key from plantera, and using it you will be able to enter the jungle temple. It might take a while to find it, but you'll be able to tell when you do.

Once you find the temple, it might take a while to get to the end of it. It is filled with deadly traps and enemies, so you should take your time and traverse it carefully.

Golem is a fairly easy boss, just make sure to get distance when needed and avoid it's projectiles.
