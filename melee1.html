Classic

Starting off

The best course of action for any class is to start building houses. Each house is required to have an area of 60 tiles, a table, and a chair. The shape of the house doesn't matter too much though.
The Mines

Your next priority is simple, mining. Most of your best weapon options will come from the caves, though it can most certainly be dangerous.
You can discover many minerals and abandonded houses that can get you many different useful items. You should also remember to upgrade your pickaxe with your resources, some minerals may be harder too mine than others.
M

Mage is good for stretegists who understand how the game is played. Many of your spells will deal lots of damage, but have a unique gimmick to spice up your run.
S

Summoner is typically seen as the hardest to utilize. The first tool you can aquire may take a bit of time, challenging your patience and fortitude. Don't let this sway you away, as this can become one of the more powerful classes in a playthrough.
