Part Eight: Mech Bosses Summoner

Boss Fight #6 Boss Fight #7 Boss Fight #8

Not much prep is needed, as you can re-use the same arena you made when you fought the Eye of Cthulhu for these next bosses. The first boss you will fight are the twins. This fight will be very difficult, seeing as there are two enemies you have to fight, but it is possible. But do not worry, if this one is too hard at the moment, you can come back later after defeating a different mech.

Another mech boss you must face is the Destroyer. Although this boss has the most Defense, it might take a while to defeat. Due to it's segments though, the damage output can be quite high.

The last mech boss you will fight is Skeletron Prime. This boss is a bit trickier, due to its 5 different points of attack, but you can create patterns of attack to avoid getting hit by him.
