Part Ten: Finale Mage

Back to the Dungeon Boss Fight #11 The Celestial Pillars Boss Fight #12 The End

The finale begins back at the dungeon, where the cultists reside. Slay them to take on your next boss.

The Cultist is a powerful boss, but shouldn't take too long to defeat. Make sure to avoid his minions, as they are extremely damaging.

After defeating the Cultist, 4 Celestial Pillars will appear. Defeat them and their peons to summon the final boss.

The final boss will be your hardest challenge yet. Be sure to note be too agressive, as getting too close when you aren't supposed to will ensure your demise.

After you defeat the Moon Lord, you will be the world's savior. Feel free to go back and chack anything you missed befoe, or mess around with the new items the Moon Lord has droped.
